Hobbies

What I Enjoy

When I'm not coding or studying, you can find me engaged in various activities that help me relax and keep a balanced lifestyle:

Badminton

I enjoy playing badminton regularly—it keeps me physically active and serves as a refreshing break from long hours of studying or coding. At IIIT Hyderabad, I’ve participated in interbatch and interhouse badminton tournaments, which not only sharpened my competitive edge but also helped me bond with peers across different years and houses. It’s my go-to sport for both fitness and fun!

Gaming

Video games have been a significant hobby for me, providing both entertainment and cognitive challenges. I enjoy various genres including action-adventure, open-world, and first-person shooters.

Games I've Played

A selection of games I've enjoyed playing over the years:

Red Dead Redemption

A Western-themed action-adventure with an immersive open world and a compelling redemption story.

Red Dead Redemption 2

A prequel with a vast detailed world, following Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang as outlaws on the run.

GTA Vice City

Set in the 1980s Miami-inspired city with a vibrant atmosphere and an iconic soundtrack.

GTA 5

Follows three protagonists with intertwining stories in the massive open world of Los Santos.

Far Cry 5

Set in Montana where a sheriff's deputy faces off against a doomsday cult in a beautiful open world.

Far Cry 6

AC Mirage

A first-person shooter franchise spanning multiple time periods with fast-paced multiplayer action.

Among Us

A social deduction game where players identify impostors sabotaging their spaceship, combining teamwork and deception.

Mystery Game

Reading Self-Help Books

I find a lot of value in self-help and personal development books that offer fresh perspectives on growth, productivity, and mindset. These books help me grow both personally and academically. Some of the most impactful ones I've read include How to Win Friends and Influence People, Rich Dad Poor Dad, Ikigai, and Atomic Habits. Each has offered unique insights into communication, purpose, habits, and financial thinking. Reading has become a daily ritual that keeps me motivated and helps me stay grounded.

Watching Documentaries

I enjoy watching documentaries that spark curiosity—mostly on YouTube. I explore a wide variety of topics, from science and technology to history, psychology, and social issues—pretty much anything that seems interesting in the moment! It's a great way to step outside my academic routine and dive into real-world concepts or ideas I hadn't considered before. These documentaries often lead me down rabbit holes of new interests and creative thinking.

Listening to Music

Music is a constant companion in my day-to-day life. I enjoy a mix of Japanese (especially anime soundtracks), English pop/rock, and old-era Hindi songs that help me relax and focus. Whether it's calming Lo-Fi during deep work or nostalgic melodies to wind down, I have playlists ready for every vibe. Music plays a big role in keeping me both productive and emotionally balanced.
