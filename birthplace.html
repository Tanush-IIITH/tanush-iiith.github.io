Birthplace

Where I'm From

I was born and raised in Faridabad, a major city in the Indian state of Haryana. Faridabad is part of the National Capital Region and is located about 25 kilometers from Delhi.

Faridabad has a rich industrial heritage and is known for its manufacturing sector. Growing up in this environment exposed me to both urban development and industrial progress, which has influenced my interest in technology and innovation.

Notable Places in Faridabad

Amrita Hospital

One of Asia's largest private hospitals, Amrita Hospital Faridabad is a state-of-the-art medical facility spread across 133 acres. It serves as a major healthcare center in the region.

Baba Farid Tomb

This sacred tomb is dedicated to Baba Farid, a 12th-century Sufi saint. The city of Faridabad is actually named after him, and his tomb is an important historical and spiritual landmark.

Badkhal Lake

Once a popular tourist spot, Badkhal Lake is surrounded by the Aravalli hills. Though the lake has dried up in recent years, the area remains a historical landmark and recreation spot.

Laxmi Narayan Mandir

A beautiful and serene Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu and Goddess Lakshmi. This temple is known for its stunning architecture, peaceful atmosphere, and religious significance to the local community.

Raja Nahar Singh Fort

This 18th-century palace in Ballabgarh is a beautiful example of Mughal architecture and an important historical site that played a role in the 1857 uprising against British rule.

Special Mention: Surajkund Crafts Mela

The Surajkund International Crafts Mela is a 15-day annual cultural festival held every February. It's one of the largest craft fairs in the world, showcasing traditional handicrafts, handlooms, and cultural performances from India and around the globe. The fair takes place around the ancient Surajkund reservoir, which was built by King Suraj Pal of the Tomar dynasty in the 10th century.
